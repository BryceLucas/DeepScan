About DeepScan

DeepScan is an AI-powered platform designed to help job seekers create professional résumés that will standout to any employer. By combining advanced text extraction and intelligent rewriting, DeepScan will refine your résumé to be ATS friendly.

Whether you are uploading an existing résumé, paste text, or use a specific target field, DeepSeek will tailor the content you provide to match your career goals and roles that you are pursuing.

Mission Statement

Our mission is simple! We want to make applying for jobs faster, easier, and more effective. We're continuously improving DeepScan, with exciting new features and enhancements coming soon, all so you can focus less on formatting, and more on landing your dream job!
