About Us

We are group of dedicated students from CSI4999, Oakland University. Members: Aidan Chapman, Bryce Lucas, Andrae Taylor, Kole Micakaj, and Michael Lafata. Our group consist of Seniors at looking to create something unique for our final few semesters at Oakland University.

Our project is a web-based chatbot application focused on resume enhancement, designed to help users improve their resumes through an intuitive and conversational AI experience. All of us have had experience using a chatbot like chatgpt or google gemini. Most users don't know how to customize these chat bots to get the best(most accurate results) results in which a project like DeepScan simplifies this process for all users.

Many of our fellow peers will be looking to enhance their resumes going into the workforce out of graduation and a tool like DeepScan is the perfect way to accomplish that. By integrating DeepSeek (an open source LLM) we can provide a cost effective and intelligent feedback agent to enhance resumes rather than paying for a service like ChatGPT. Users can upload their resumes and input a target job title which DeepScan uses to tailor its output for the desired sector the user specializes in.
